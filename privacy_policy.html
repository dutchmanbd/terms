When you log in using Google Sign-In through Firebase Authentication, we may collect:

b) Symptom & Health Information

We may collect:

  • Symptoms entered by users

  • General health-related questions

This information is used only to generate AI-based responses and improve app functionality.

c) Device & Usage Information

We may automatically collect:

  • Crash logs
